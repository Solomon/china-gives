Guangdong – Land of Charity

The southern province is home to 21 philanthropists, making it the most generous region. He Xiangjian, founder of electrical appliance manufacturer Midea, leads with ¥410 million donation in the 12 months period. Top 100 philanthropists are located in 21 municipalities/provinces. Guangdong is followed by Beijing (16), Zhejiang (11) and Fujian (8).

Real Estate Gives

China’s booming property market has minted the most philanthropists. There are 32 individuals whose core business is building houses, nearly twice as the next most generous sector: manufacturing (18). Consumer (15) and technology (14) sector rank the third and fourth, respectively.

Education Takes

Education gets the lion’s share with 81 individuals giving to schools or students. Eight Chinese universities received donations of at least ¥100 million yuan. Poverty alleviation and social welfare is the second most active sector (62), followed by disaster relief (35).

Government - Trusted Sector

Our research shows that philanthropists tend to trust their money to charity organization from public sector better than those from private sector. About 55 % of all donations in 2015 went to goverment related charity organizations.

On the other hand, 2015 showed huge increase in number of NGOs and private charity organizations.

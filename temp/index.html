Government - Trusted Sector

Our research shows that philanthropists tend to trust their money to charity organization from public sector better than those from private sector. About 55 % of all donations in 2015 went to goverment related charity organizations.

On the other hand, 2015 showed huge increase in number of NGOs and private charity organizations.
